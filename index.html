Why aura ?

Aura is a decoupled, event-driven architecture for developing widget-based applications.

Giving your application a structure that stays safe and scales gracefully along your application requires expertise and foresight.

Aura introduces proven patterns and best practices for developing maintainable applications, while giving you greater control over widget-based applications.

Aura gives you complete control of a widget's lifecycle, allowing developers to dynamically start, stop, reload and clean-up parts of their application as needed.
